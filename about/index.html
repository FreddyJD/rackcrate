    What is RackCrate?

    RackCrate is a joint venture of dedicated, colocation and hardware providers. We began as two man team of passionate developers and quickly realized that there was a large market for hosting services in the IT industry and slowly began adding a variety of services including dedicated servers, colocation hosting, and refurbished servers.

    We’ve been fortunate to have a committed team that has worked together to evolve a dream into an reality.

    The most effective hosting solutions are built on a strength and balance of technologies, expertise, and customer commitment. From our humble beginnings in the gaming industry, we’ve been committed to delivering the very best to serve the needs of our customers.


    Customer Service

    From CEO to support technician, our team is made up of experts in network performance who are dedicated to delivering what you need, when you need it.

    We offer you support every step of the way. Beginning with the first conversation, we work with you to understand your business systems, infrastructure requirements and budget, then design a colocation solution that fits your needs today, and in the future. Once your custom solution is designed, our on-site network experts work, person-to-person, with your team to ensure a smooth transition—from migration planning to remote management set-up.
